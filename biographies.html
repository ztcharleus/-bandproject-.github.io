About the Members

Oliver Sim

Oliver Sim, 26, is one of the lead singers and the bass player of the The xx. Sim was raised in a council flat in South London by his mom (a social worker) and his dad (a charity administrator). Both parents were supportive and encouraged Sim to express himself, one way being through music. Sim became friends with Romy Madley-Croft, the other lead singer, in nursery school due to their parents being close friends. Both extremly shy, during their time in High School they would exchange song ideas through email. Sim was not interested in taking their music any further having other plans for his life. However after being discovered on myspace, their music careers took off.

Romy Madley-Croft

Romy Madley-Croft, 27, makes up 1/2 of "The xx"'s vocal power. Romy grew up in southwest London, the only child of a primary school teacher mother and a father who worked in a library. When Romy was only 11 her mother died of a brain haemorrhage and she was sent to live with her aunt, uncle, and their daughter. Romy stating "I think in that moment, I went from being a young 11 year old to an older 11 year old. I awlays felt older that I was." Shortly after the release of their first album, Romys father also passed away, reportedly from complications arising from alcoholism. Romy states that music and preforming shows is a way for her to express herself and help her deal with feelings of greif.

Jamie XX

Jamie Smith also known as Jamie xx is the producer and beat creator for The xx. Smith beleives that his interest in electronic dance music might have come from his family. Two of his uncles were DJs. One of them was the first to teach 10 year old Jamie how to mix two records on a pair of turntables. Along the years during his career with The XX and his own solo debut, Smith has become one of electronic musics most respective figures with production credit on top artists music such as Drake.
